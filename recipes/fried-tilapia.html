Pan Fried Tilapia

Description

Beautifully golden on the outside and perfectly flakey and moist inside, this tilapia recipe comes together super quickly. It only requires a handful of healthy and flavorful ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Butter
  • Paprika
  • Tilapia
  • Olive Oil
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Season tilapia fillets with salt, pepper, paprika, and garlic powder.
  2. Place the flour onto a plate or shallow baking dish and then dredge each filet in the flour to lightly coat both sides of the fish.
  3. Shake off any excess flour from the filets.
  4. Heat the olive oil and butter in a skillet over medium-high heat and add the fillets 1 or 2 at a time, depending on the size of your pan.
  5. Cook the fillets for 2 to 3 minutes per side until golden brown
  6. Garnish with a squeeze of lemon juice and chopped parsley.
